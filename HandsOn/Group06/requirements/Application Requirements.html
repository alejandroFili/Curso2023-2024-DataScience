Application Requirements:

The purpose of our application is to offer our users different parking lots in Agra close to their location. To carry out this service, we linked two datasets(parking locations and roads in Agra).

To offer our user a more complete service, our application could potentially offer different information such as opening and closing time or even the cost per hour. Furthermore, users will be able to filter the parking lots depending on their proximity(radius search) and their opening hours.
