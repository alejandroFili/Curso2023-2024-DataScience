https://data.europa.eu/data/datasets/5f6582ec1c74c6550e8021e3?locale=es

In order to match some of the entities described in the ‘parking-paris’ dataset. We have chosen a dataset with information on the streets in París.

https://opendata.paris.fr/explore/dataset/adresse_paris/information/

                                                            Matching Entities

As we looked into the first dataset, we found various entities such as the streets where the parking lots were located. So, the ‘adresse_paris’ dataset was very suitable in order to locate those entities.
